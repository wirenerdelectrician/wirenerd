About

Built to make every electrician better.

WireNerd exists to make electrical work genuinely clear — and to put that clarity in front of as many electricians as we possibly can.

Our mission

Our goal is simple: reach as many electricians as we can, and help them build successful, lasting careers. Whether you're an apprentice studying for your first license, a journeyman sharpening your edge, or someone trying to make sense of a circuit on the jobsite at 2 a.m., WireNerd is here to make it click — in plain language, with clear diagrams, and hands-on practice you can actually use.

Why WireNerd

Most electrical training is either too academic or too vague. We do it differently:

  • Plain language — concepts explained the way a good lead explains them on the job, not the way a textbook buries them.
  • Learn it, then build it — every concept pairs with our interactive Wiring Sandbox, so you wire it, energize it, and troubleshoot it yourself.
  • Field-tested and accurate — content comes from real experience in the panel, and accuracy is non-negotiable.
  • Free at the core — the fundamentals stay free, because the whole trade is better when more people truly understand it.

What you'll find today

  • Lessons — clear walkthroughs of control wiring, motor control, PLCs, and troubleshooting.
  • Build Guides — step-by-step panel builds, from first wire to final test.
  • Wiring Sandbox — an interactive trainer where you build real circuits, energize them, and hunt down faults with a virtual meter.
  • Components Reference — what each device is, how it behaves, and where it's used.
  • Become a Journeyman — a study guide, topic practice, and timed mock exams for exam prep.
  • Live Classes — instructor-led sessions (coming soon).

Where we're headed

This is just the beginning. We're building toward complete coverage of the trade — eventually spanning:

  • Residential wiring
  • Commercial systems
  • Industrial controls
  • Low-voltage applications
  • Intro to PLCs
  • Solar & renewable energy

As we grow, we're also working to build relationships with colleges, training programs, and institutions — so WireNerd can support electricians at every stage, from the classroom to the field.

Our vision

We want WireNerd to become a respected brand and a trusted name in electrical education. We're committed to helping the next generation of electricians develop the knowledge and skills they need to succeed in the industry — and to raising the bar for how the trade is taught.

Want to be part of it? Jump into a lesson, build something in the Sandbox, or send feedback. And if you ever catch a technical error, tell us — accuracy matters here. Reach us on the contact page.
