About

Built by an electrician, for electricians.

WireNerd exists to make industrial electrical control wiring genuinely clear — the way it should have been taught the first time.

WireNerd is run by Bryan Barrios, a licensed Utah journeyman electrician. After years on the tools and in the panel, the goal here is simple: explain control wiring, motor control, relay logic, and the code in plain language, then let you practice it hands-on.

What you'll find here

  • Lessons — clear, no-nonsense walkthroughs of control wiring, motor control, PLCs, and troubleshooting.
  • Build Guides — step-by-step panel builds with parts and wiring, from first wire to final test.
  • Wiring Sandbox — an interactive trainer where you build real control circuits, energize them, and troubleshoot faults with a virtual meter.
  • Components Reference — what each device is, how it behaves, and where it's used.
  • Become a Journeyman — a study guide, topic practice, and timed mock exams for journeyman exam prep.
  • Classes — live, instructor-led sessions (coming soon).

Where we stand

WireNerd is an independent study and exam-prep resource — not a school, training academy, or licensing authority, and not affiliated with NFPA or any government agency. Everything here is meant to help you understand the trade and prepare; it isn't a substitute for formal training, the adopted code, or a licensed professional.
