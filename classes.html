Good to know

When do classes start?

Dates are being finalized now. The fastest way to find out is to join the early-access list above — you'll get the schedule the moment it's set.

How much will they cost?

Pricing is per seat and will be posted with the schedule. List members get an early-bird rate before general registration.

What if I can't attend live?

Every session is recorded. If you're registered and miss it, you'll get the replay to watch on your own time.

Do I need anything special?

Just Zoom (free) and something to take notes with. Any handouts or worksheets are emailed to you ahead of the session.

Are these only for Utah?

The NEC exam-prep class is built around Utah's journeyman exam, but the controls, prints, PLC, and troubleshooting classes apply to electricians anywhere.
